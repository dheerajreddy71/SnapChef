TITLE : SnapChef

ABSTRACT :

People like food photos because they love food. Every meal has a story in its recipe, but just looking at a picture doesn't tell you how to make it. So, we are introducing SnapChef that figures out recipes from food pictures. It guesses what ingredients are in the food and how to cook it, without needing to follow a strict order. This helps people understand recipes better just by looking at pictures.
